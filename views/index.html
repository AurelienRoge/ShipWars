Rules of the game

The goal of the game is simple you must sink all your opponent's boats before he does.


First of all, you start by placing your boats on the game grid. Now the game can start, each of you takes a turn to shoot at one of the slots on the grid. If one of the boats is on the square where you shot the boat is hit and you can continue shooting. Otherwise the boat is not hit and you pass your turn to the other player. If the entire boat is hit, the boat is sunk, so you can try to shoot for another boat. If all the ships are sunk you have won the game.



To hit the opponent's ships you have access to different weapons during the game:


- A missile that can be used every turn and hits the square clicked by the player

- The radar which can be used only once per game, it allows to indicate the squares around the clicked square, which means 9 squares.

- The torpedo can be used once per game if a ship is revealed and if you have two or less squares left to discover you can destroy it directly

- The fragment bomb can also be used once per game, it touches 5 squares and forms a cross around the clicked location.
